BUNGLOW HOUSE PLANS,FLOOR PLANS & DESIGNS

If you love the charm of craftsman house plans and are working with a small lot,a bumglow house plan might be your best bet.
Bunglow floor plan designs are typically simple, compact and longer than they are wide.Also, like their craftsman
cousin, bunglow house designs tend to sport cute curb appeal by way of a wide front porch
supported by tapered of paired columns and low slung rooflines.
